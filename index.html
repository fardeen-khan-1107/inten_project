OUR FEATURED PRODUCTS
Hacksaw frame

A metal frame with a handle and blade holder, used for supporting and tensioning hacksaw blades, enabling controlled cutting of various materials such as metal, plastic, or wood in fabrication, construction, automotive repair, and DIY projects.

Hacksaw blade

A long, narrow blade with serrated teeth along one edge, designed for cutting metal, plastic, or other materials, compatible with hacksaw frames for precise and efficient cutting in fabrication, construction, automotive repair, and DIY projects.

Chiesel

A sharp-edged hand tool used for cutting, shaping, or carving hard materials such as wood, stone, or metal, featuring a blade at one end and a handle for precision and control, essential in woodworking, masonry, and sculpture.

Barrelpump

A manual or electric pump designed for transferring liquids such as oil, fuel, water, or chemicals from barrels or containers to tanks or other vessels, offering efficient and reliable fluid transfer solutions in industrial, automotive, agricultural, and marine applications.

Plier

A versatile hand tool with jaws for gripping, cutting, bending, and twisting various materials such as wires and small objects, essential for a wide range of tasks in construction, electrical work, automotive repair, and crafting.

About Company

Sukhleen International

based in Ludhiana, Punjab, India, is a manufacturer and trader of a wide range of hand tools, power tools, tractor parts, gardening tools, and other cutting tools. The company's Quality Management System has been assessed and certified to meet the ISO 9001:2015 standard, ensuring adherence to rigorous quality standards in its operations. Sukhleen International's commitment to quality is further demonstrated through its successful completion of surveillance audits, ensuring continuous compliance with certification requirements. Accredited by EGAC and endorsed by SRK System and Services Pvt. Ltd., an ISO certification body, the company upholds international standards of excellence in its products and services.Sukhleen International exemplifies a dedication to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction in its industry.
